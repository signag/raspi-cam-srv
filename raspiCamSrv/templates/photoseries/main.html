Here, you can specify a series as an exposure series.

Since Raspberry Pi cameras have fixed or manual aperture, only exposure time (shutter speed) and analogue gain (instead of ISO) can be varied.

Be aware that the live stream will be affected by changing control parameters
While the series is active, Automatic Exposure (AE) and Automatic White Balance (AWB) will be disabled.
It is recommended to activate these parameters before the series is started.
